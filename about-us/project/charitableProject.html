Charitable Dispensary Projects

Charitable Dispensary Project by RBM

Charitable Dispensary in Gottigere and HSIS is a result of continuous & honest efforts by Rotary Bangalore Midtown. Here we offer great services to poor & needy on a charitable basis at very less cost. Our hospital is recognized by Govt. of Karnataka and through which we offer multiple insurance schemes & cashless services to the people. Team of expert doctors, efficient support staff, and advanced equipment enable us to handle complex medical issues and treat patients with special care has built trust in the people resulting in a brilliant success in our mission of building a leading charitable institution and one of the most trusted medical care centers around.

At Gottigere an ongoing project for almost a decade now Continuing to treat the rural sick. Around 1400 patients per month are treated at a budgeted outlay of approx. 12 lakhs per annum.

We also had a Free Heart Checkup that was organized in association with Dr.Rahul Shetty, a Cardiology Specialist. A total of 54 patients underwent Tests. Seven Gottigere Rotary high school teachers also underwent Tests. Even a Free Diabetic camp was organized and a total of 89 people were benefited.

At HSIS dispensary at Mosque Road, the budgeted outlay of around 60 lakhs. Around 7500 patients are treated in one month. Free Diabetic camp along with a talk on Diabetes prevention by Abbot was organized and 150 people were benefited.

We have also Organised many health camps in the previous year like eye checkups, bone density checkups, blood donation camps, breast cancer awareness, and many more. We have a mission to make sure everyone gets the basic health facilities. We thank all our members for their continuous support which is helping us to keep up the noble work and in achieving the mission. Our Vision is providing inclusive health services to poor and economically backward people at the most affordable price.
