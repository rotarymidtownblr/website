Urban Forest Project

Urban Forestation by RBM

THE CITY OF BANGALORE HAS BEEN SUBJECTED TO RAPID, INTENSE URBANIZATION IN THE LAST DECADE, PUTTING THE CITY'S LIMITED RESOURCES AND INFRASTRUCTURE UNDER IMMENSE PRESSURE APTLY CALLED 'GARDEN CITY , BANGALORE WAS PROUD OF ITS THRIVING AND EXEMPLARY GREEN COVER. SADLY IN A MATTER OF JUST A FEW YEARS, IT IS FAST TURNING INTO A CEMENT CONCRETE JUNGLE. CITIZENS HAVE WATCHED HELPLESSLY AS THE LUSH ANCIENT TREES GET MERCILESSLY CHOPPED - LEAVING BEHIND A BARREN, OXYGEN STARVED LANDSCAPE! WE SAY, "NO MORE!!!" LET US TAKE CHARGE AND PLEDGE TO REVERSE THIS DEVASTATION!

BENEFITS OF URBAN FORESTS
  • Cleaner, Cooler Air

  • Reduction of Pollution
