Health & Hygiene Project

Health & hygienisation by RBM

As a part of community service project and one of the Rotary’s thrust area of girland women health and hygiene, Midtown Club Ann’s planned to distributeHealth & Hygiene Kits (sanitary napkins) to underprivileged girls and destitute women.

Each girl / women were given one hamper of 25 packs for a year requirement.Each pack consists of 6 napkin,One case (25 pack ×6) contains total 150 sanitary napkin which will be sufficient for a girl child for herentire years requirement.

A video along with a talk on Hygiene was also presented at these schools andinstitutions. A big thanks to our Asst. Governor RtnRameshkumar for sourcing the hygienematerial at a very subsidized cost. Each Box costed Rs.350/-

The First phase of distribution started on 6 th Mar 2020, Anns of Midtown got together and gave shape to another mega project. It was a very meaningful and satisfying experience at the project execution:
